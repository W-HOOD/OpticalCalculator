Task 1: Plotting Refractive index

Choose whether to plot the refractive index of Crown glass using the sellmeier equations, or of water using the Empirical Formula.

Sellmeier Equation:
\( n^2(\lambda) = 1 + \sum_{i=1}^{3} \frac{B_i \lambda^2}{\lambda^2 - C_i} \)
Empirical Formula:
\( \left(n^2 - 1\right)^{-2} = 1.731 - 0.261 f^2 \)

X maximum must be greater than X minimum!
